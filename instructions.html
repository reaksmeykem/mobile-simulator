📱 Mobile Simulator Setup

Follow these steps to complete the installation of your Chrome extension.

🎨 Step 1: Generate Icons

1 Open the icon generator:
  • Open create-icons.html in your browser
  • You'll see three icon previews (16×16, 48×48, 128×128)
2 Download the icons:
  • Click the "Download All Icons" button
  • Three PNG files will be downloaded: icon16.png, icon48.png, icon128.png
3 Save to icons folder:
  • Move the downloaded files to mobile-simulator/icons/
  • Make sure the filenames match exactly: icon16.png, icon48.png, icon128.png

🔧 Step 2: Load Extension in Chrome

1 Open Chrome Extensions:
  • Navigate to chrome://extensions/
  • Or click the puzzle icon → "Manage Extensions"
2 Enable Developer Mode:
  • Toggle "Developer mode" in the top right corner
3 Load the extension:
  • Click "Load unpacked"
  • Select the mobile-simulator folder
  • The extension should now appear in your toolbar!

✅ Step 3: Test It Out

1 Open a website:
  • Navigate to any regular website (e.g., google.com)
2 Click the extension icon:
  • Click the 📱 Mobile Simulator icon in your toolbar
  • Select a device from the dropdown (e.g., "iPhone 17 Pro")
3 Enjoy the simulator:
  • The page will display in a realistic mobile mockup
  • Try rotating, toggling the frame, or switching devices
⚠️ Important: The extension only works on regular websites. It won't work on:
  • chrome:// pages
  • chrome-extension:// pages
  • edge:// or about: pages
🎉 Features:
  • 16 different devices (iPhone, Samsung, Pixel, iPad)
  • Realistic device mockups with notch/punch-hole cameras
  • Rotate between portrait and landscape
  • Toggle device frame on/off
  • Organized device selector by category

🔄 After Making Changes

If you modify the code:

  1. Go to chrome://extensions/
  2. Click the reload icon (🔄) on Mobile Simulator
  3. Refresh your test webpage
